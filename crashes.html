-What design ellemnts did i focus on?-

To build a racing drone you first need to select the components you want to use. You must choose carefully as not all components work together well, for example if you pug a five-cell battery in to a board that can only take the voltage of a four cell then you will have a big fire this has happened to me many times. the drone consists of these components that you will have to solder and put together. flight controller (FC) ESC’s (electric speed controllers) motors frame camera VTX (video transmitter) receiver antenna depending on how well u select your parts and how skilfully you put them together is how well and fast your quad will fly. My fastest quad has reached over 100kph in a straight line and it was very very loud!
